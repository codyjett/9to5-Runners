9to5 RUNNERS — LOCKED MASTER RULES

HIGHEST PRIORITY
- Never change the framework of an approved page during a rebuild.
- Do not change HTML structure, CSS, layout, spacing, font sizes, shadows,
  borders, header, footer, navigation, search, JavaScript, or responsive behavior.
- Use the approved page files byte-for-byte as the master framework.
- Only change requested content inside existing components.

HOMEPAGE
- Latest published review is the hero.
- Latest Reviews excludes the hero.
- Up to 9 reviews, 3 per row.
- No Featured Stories.
- No placeholders.
- No More Reviews button.
- Do not add helper/developer notes to the visible page.

SHOES PAGE
- Preserve the approved hero, filters, review-row layout, footer, and CSS.
- Add new reviews only by cloning the approved review-row structure.
- Remove placeholders only.

RELEASE
- Replace approved master files directly.
- Do not regenerate approved pages.
- Audit every local link before delivery.

ARCHIVE PAGINATION
- Shoe and Gear archive pages display no more than 25 reviews per page.
- Preserve the approved archive framework.
- Add pagination only when more than 25 reviews exist.
