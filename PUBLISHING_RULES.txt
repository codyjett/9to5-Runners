9to5 RUNNERS PUBLISHING RULES

HOMEPAGE
- The newest published review is always the top hero.
- Keep the approved homepage hero template unchanged.
- Update image, title, category, date, description, and link together.
- Latest Reviews is the only review grid.
- Latest Reviews excludes the hero review.
- Show up to 9 real reviews, 3 per row.
- No placeholders.
- No More Reviews button.
- Latest Reviews, Latest From YouTube, and Shop by Brand use matching heading typography.

SHOES / GEAR ARCHIVES
- Add each published review in the existing archive card format.
- Show published dates.
- Remove placeholder cards.
- Preserve the approved archive hero and filters.

REVIEW PAGES
- Nimbus is the master shoe-review template.
- Use the approved header, footer, tech specs, image sizing, typography, and spacing.
- Use permanent Cody, Blake, and Eric profile graphics when those reviewers participate.
- Internal review IDs never display publicly.
